3M Scotch-Weld DP8805NS Low Odor Acrylic Adhesive

The 3M Scotch-Weld DP8805NS is a high-performance acrylic adhesive designed for safe and efficient bonding in tough environments. Its low odor and non-flammable formulation make it an excellent choice for industrial, manufacturing, and scientific settings where air quality and safety are critical.

Key Features

  • Engineered for strength and speed, this adhesive sets quickly with a 4-minute work life and achieves structural strength in just 9 minutes.
  • Robust shear and impact resistance ensure long-lasting bonds that hold up in demanding conditions.
  • Specialized to bond challenging materials such as powder coats, plastics, and oily surfaces, where conventional adhesives may not perform optimally.
  • Includes precision spacer beads for precise and consistent bond line thickness control, improving overall application quality.
  • Offers an 18-month shelf life, making it a reliable and cost-effective option for inventory management.

Perfect For

Ideal for applications in automotive, manufacturing, and industrial fields, the DP8805NS ensures secure, high-quality bonds without the hassle of prolonged curing times. Whether you're repairing, assembling, or working in confined spaces, this adhesive delivers dependable performance and exceptional safety.

Why Choose 3M Scotch-Weld DP8805NS?

With its fast drying time and strong, durable bond, this product is a trusted solution for professionals looking to enhance productivity and achieve high-quality results. The combination of safety, speed, and strength makes it a top choice for demanding tasks that require precision and reliability.
