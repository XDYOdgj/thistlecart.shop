DEWALT AC100+ Gold Quik-Shot Acrylic Epoxy Adhesive with Red Devil 3989 Caulk Gun

Whether you're working on high-strength concrete or masonry projects, this durable adhesive kit is built for professionals who demand reliability and efficiency. The DEWALT AC100+ Gold Quik-Shot Acrylic Epoxy Adhesive is specifically designed to bond threaded rod and steel reinforcing bar into hardened surfaces, offering consistent strength and performance in even the harshest environments.

Its powerful formula ensures long-lasting results, making it ideal for both low and high strength applications. This is the perfect choice for those looking for a high-strength bonding solution that can withstand extreme conditions and maintain its integrity over time.

Complementing the adhesive is the Red Devil 3989 9\" Extreme Duty Caulk Gun. Engineered for industrial and scientific use, this caulk gun boasts a 26:1 thrust ratio that makes thick material extrusion effortless. With 950 pounds of force, it tackles the toughest jobs with precision and control.

The ergonomic design of the caulk gun features a rubber trigger and push release, providing better grip and comfort during extended use. The no drip mechanism guarantees smooth application, while the built-in seal puncture tool simplifies the process. These features make this kit a reliable and user-friendly choice for professionals looking to improve efficiency and reduce waste on the job site.
