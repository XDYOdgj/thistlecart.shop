BSTFLEX Self Sealing Silicone Tape

BSTFLEX Self Sealing Silicone Tape is designed for all your waterproof and leak-proof sealing needs. With its premium-quality silicone rubber construction, this heavy-duty tape bonds instantly when wrapped and pressed, forming a durable, long-lasting seal that holds strong in even the most demanding conditions.

The clear finish ensures visibility, making it perfect for sealing electrical cables, outdoor equipment, and other applications where an unobtrusive, secure seal is essential. Whether you're working on a vehicle, in the garden, or in an industrial setting, this versatile tape is your go-to solution for reliable, waterproof performance.

Featuring a 1-inch width and 36-foot length, this tape offers extensive surface coverage without sacrificing strength or flexibility. Its easy-tear liner makes application simple and efficient, while the high resistance to heat, UV, oil, and solvents ensures long-term use in various environments. With standards compliance including UL 510 and ISO 9001, BSTFLEX delivers safety and reliability you can trust.
