Superclear Table Top Epoxy

Superclear Table Top Epoxy is the perfect choice for anyone looking to create professional-grade surfaces with unmatched clarity and shine. Whether you're working on a DIY bar top, a polished wooden countertop, or a sleek glass surface, this 256 oz kit delivers a flawless finish that stands the test of time.

Featuring a food-safe formula compliant with 21 CFR 175.300 and ASTM D-4236, this epoxy is ideal for use in kitchens, bars, and other environments where cleanliness and safety are essential. Its UV-resistant properties ensure that your projects retain their vibrant, clear appearance without yellowing or clouding, even after years of exposure.

The smooth, bubble-free application makes it easy to achieve a professional outcome, whether you're a hobbyist or a seasoned professional. Add color and style to your surface with ease using compatible coloring agents like pigment powders or alcohol inks, which blend seamlessly without compromising the epoxy's natural clarity.

Key Features

  • Food-Grade Safe: Perfect for use on surfaces that come into contact with food.
  • Ultra-Gloss Finish: Creates a mirror-like surface that enhances visual appeal.
  • UV Resistant: Prevents yellowing and maintains clarity over time.
  • Seamless Application: Ensures a smooth, bubble-free finish on any surface.
  • Impact Resistant: Provides a durable finish that withstands daily wear and tear.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced craftsman, this premium epoxy is designed to deliver exceptional results. With its high-gloss, UV-resistant properties and compatibility with coloring agents, it's a versatile and reliable option for all your surface coating projects.
