  Starborn CMNTFAST Fasteners are specially designed to tackle the unique challenges of cement board applications. With their epoxy coating, these fasteners ensure a secure, long-lasting bond to the substrate. The cross-cut knurl pattern effectively grinds the cement board, significantly reducing cracking and mushrooming issues that often occur with standard fasteners. The serrated ribs under the head provide a flush, even surface, making them ideal for professional installations. The patented drive mechanism is engineered to eliminate cam-outs and wobble, ensuring a smooth and consistent installation process. These fasteners are perfect for use with popular cement board products like Hardiebacker, Durock, and Wonderboard.
