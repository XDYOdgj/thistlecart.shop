Epoxy International's Carbon-Bond 61

Epoxy International's Carbon-Bond 61 is a carbon-filled, room temperature cure epoxy adhesive engineered for superior electrical conductivity. Perfect for applications where reliable performance is essential, it ensures a continuous flow of conductivity even after curing, delivering consistent results for electronic components, PCBs, and other conductive materials.

This advanced adhesive eliminates the need for heat, making it ideal for delicate substrates and time-sensitive projects. With a high adhesion strength, it forms durable bonds on a wide range of surfaces, including metals, ceramics, and glass. Its broad operating temperature range offers unmatched versatility, allowing it to function effectively in diverse environments.

Key Features

  • Electrically conductive for use in sensitive electronic applications
  • Room temperature curing for convenience and time-saving
  • High adhesion strength for durable bonds on various surfaces
  • Carbon-filled formulation ensures consistent conductivity
  • Wide operating temperature range for versatility in use

Whether you're in electronics, automotive, aerospace, or industrial settings, Carbon-Bond 61 is designed to meet your demanding requirements with confidence. Its user-friendly 10:1 mix ratio and fast curing time make it an efficient choice for precision equipment like circuit boards and sensors that rely on electrical integrity.

Why Choose Carbon-Bond 61?

With unmatched conductivity, durable bonding, and a wide range of applications, Carbon-Bond 61 is the perfect adhesive for professionals seeking reliability and performance. It combines safety, efficiency, and strength to provide a long-lasting solution in electronics and industrial environments.
