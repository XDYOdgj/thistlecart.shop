Epoxy Potting Compound for Electronic Protection

This high-performance epoxy potting compound is specifically crafted for use in electronic applications where precision and durability are essential. Its unique thixotropic properties ensure minimal flow during application, delivering a clean and controlled process that minimizes the risk of unwanted leaks or drips. Designed for use with sensitive components, it can encapsulate up to 8 cubic inches without causing significant exotherm, making it a safe and reliable choice for a variety of environments.

The opaque black finish not only enhances the professional appearance of your finished product but also effectively obscures the internal circuit design, adding an extra layer of security. Whether you're working on printed circuit boards or plastic enclosures, this compound offers exceptional insulation against AC and DC voltages, ensuring both electrical safety and long-term performance. It cures at room temperature or with heat, allowing for flexible application depending on your project's needs.

With a 2:1 mix ratio by weight or volume, this epoxy is easy to use and provides consistent results, ideal for both DIY enthusiasts and seasoned professionals. Its non-conductive nature ensures electrical safety, while the waterproof properties make it suitable for applications requiring continuous immersion. From industrial equipment to automotive electronics and beyond, this compound is your go-to solution for comprehensive protection and reliable operation.
