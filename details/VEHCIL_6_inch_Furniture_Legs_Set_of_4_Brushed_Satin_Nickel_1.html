Upgrade Your Furniture with Sleek and Durable Legs

These modern furniture legs are designed to transform the look of your existing pieces. Featuring a tapered, brushed satin nickel finish, they add a refined touch of elegance to your sofa, bed, cabinet, or dresser without overwhelming the minimalist style. Whether you're looking to refresh your home decor or enhance the aesthetics of your furniture, these legs offer a subtle yet striking upgrade.

Built to last, these legs come with heavy-duty construction that can support up to 1600 lbs. Formed under 100 tons of high-pressure stamping, they are incredibly strong and capable of holding up to the demands of both residential and commercial use. Their stylish metallic texture complements mid-century and industrial designs perfectly, bringing a touch of sophistication to any space.

Perfect for DIY furniture projects, these legs are easy to install. They come with 16 screws and pre-drilled holes, ensuring a smooth setup for even beginners. The included rubber pads at the base provide protection against scratches and reduce noise, making them both practical and stylish.
