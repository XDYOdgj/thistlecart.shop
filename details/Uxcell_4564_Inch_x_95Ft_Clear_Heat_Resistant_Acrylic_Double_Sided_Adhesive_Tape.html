Premium Double-Sided Acrylic Adhesive Tape

This high-performance clear heat resistant acrylic tape is engineered for precision and reliability in diverse applications. With a 1mm thickness, it offers a robust yet flexible bond that's ideal for touch screen repairs, ensuring a secure fit without leaving behind any sticky residue. The transparent finish provides a clean, seamless look perfect for delicate electronics and polished surfaces.

The red protective film included makes it easy to apply and remove the tape, reducing the risk of damaging your work. Whether you're working on a smartphone repair, installing an LCD panel, or creating intricate 3D cards, this tape delivers consistent performance across paper, cloth, wood, foam, glass, metal, and plastic surfaces.
