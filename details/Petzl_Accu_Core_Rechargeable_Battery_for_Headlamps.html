  The Petzl Accu Core is a powerful, rechargeable battery that delivers reliable performance for all your outdoor lighting needs. With a high-capacity 1250 mAh lithium-ion design, it ensures your headlamps stay lit longer than traditional batteries. The built-in USB port allows for convenient recharging on the go, while the charge indicator light gives you a quick glance at the battery status. This makes it an ideal choice for hikers, campers, and adventurers who rely on consistent lighting in challenging conditions.
