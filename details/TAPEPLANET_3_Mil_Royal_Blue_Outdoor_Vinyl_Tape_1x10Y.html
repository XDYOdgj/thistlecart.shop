TAPEPLANET 3 Mil Royal Blue Outdoor Vinyl Tape

This high-quality outdoor vinyl tape is engineered with a calendered intermediate film and a robust permanent acrylic adhesive. It's designed for both indoor and outdoor applications, offering reliable performance no matter the environment. The semi-gloss surface enhances readability for signs and banners while delivering a sleek, professional appearance. With a 3 mil thickness, it combines strength with flexibility, enabling it to stick to uneven or curved surfaces without losing durability. Ideal for temporary signage, sealing, or securing, this tape is built to last and handle tough conditions.

Durable for Outdoor and Industrial Use

Perfect for outdoor signage and promotional materials, this outdoor vinyl tape is weather-resistant and can endure harsh conditions like rain, snow, and extreme temperatures. It's also chemical-resistant, withstanding exposure to mild acids, alkalis, and oils, which makes it highly versatile for industrial and construction settings. The easy-to-read semi-gloss finish ensures clear visibility for labeling and marking purposes. Available in 1 inch x 10 yards, this tape is designed for long-term use, offering up to 5 years of outdoor durability. A must-have for professionals who need reliable, long-lasting adhesive solutions.
