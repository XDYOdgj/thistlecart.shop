JQK Matte Black Towel Bar

Transform the look and functionality of your bathroom with the JQK Matte Black Towel Bar. This sleek and modern towel holder is designed to make your daily routine more organized and efficient. Made from durable 304 stainless steel, it offers long-lasting performance without any risk of rust, ensuring your towels stay fresh and dry.

The sturdy construction of this wall-mounted towel bar features a thick 0.8mm tube and 2mm posts that provide strong support for your towels. This eliminates the need for folding or stacking, giving you more space and a cleaner appearance. Its matte black finish effortlessly blends with any bathroom decor, adding a touch of contemporary elegance.

The 2-pack set offers versatile options, making it suitable for both single and dual towel use in residential or commercial settings.

Easy to assemble, this stainless steel towel bar comes with all the necessary hardware and a clear installation guide.
